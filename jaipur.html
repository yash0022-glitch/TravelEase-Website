About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famously known as the Pink City. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, it is a UNESCO World Heritage City. Jaipur is renowned for its forts, palaces, bustling bazaars, and rich traditions. From the majestic Amber Fort to the intricate Hawa Mahal, Jaipur offers a royal experience to every traveler.

Best Places to Visit in Jaipur

Amber Fort

A majestic fort with stunning architecture and elephant rides.

Hawa Mahal

The Palace of Winds, famous for its unique honeycomb design.

City Palace

A royal residence showcasing Rajput and Mughal architecture.

Jantar Mantar

A UNESCO site with astronomical instruments built in the 18th century.

Jal Mahal

A palace floating in the middle of Man Sagar Lake, perfect for photography.
