Top Summer Picks ☀️

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Manali is a paradise for summer travelers with snow-capped peaks, adventure sports, and lush valleys. Route: Delhi → Chandigarh → Manali (by road or flight).

  • Solang Valley – Paragliding & skiing
  • Rohtang Pass – Snow adventures
  • Hadimba Temple – Ancient wooden temple

Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir

Ladakh offers breathtaking landscapes, monasteries, and Pangong Lake. Route: Delhi → Leh (flight) or Manali → Leh (road trip).

  • Pangong Lake – Iconic blue waters
  • Magnetic Hill – Optical illusion spot
  • Thiksey Monastery – Tibetan Buddhist culture

Ooty, Tamil Nadu

Known as the “Queen of Hill Stations,” Ooty has tea gardens, botanical gardens, and Nilgiri hills. Route: Bangalore → Mysore → Ooty (road/train).

  • Ooty Lake – Boating & picnic spot
  • Botanical Garden – Exotic plants
  • Doddabetta Peak – Highest point in Nilgiris

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Famous for its toy train, tea estates, and Kanchenjunga views. Route: Kolkata → Siliguri → Darjeeling (train/road).

  • Tiger Hill – Sunrise view of Kanchenjunga
  • Darjeeling Himalayan Railway – UNESCO heritage toy train
  • Tea Estates – World-famous Darjeeling tea

Suggested 4-Day Summer Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Local Exploration

Check-in at hotel, visit local markets, enjoy evening walk.

Day 2: Adventure & Sightseeing

Head to Solang Valley (Manali) or Pangong Lake (Ladakh) for adventure activities.

Day 3: Cultural & Scenic Spots

Explore monasteries in Ladakh, botanical gardens in Ooty, or tea estates in Darjeeling.

Day 4: Relax & Departure

Enjoy leisure time, shopping for souvenirs, and prepare for return journey.

Summer Travel Tips

  • ✔ Carry light cotton clothes and sunscreen.
  • ✔ Book hotels in advance (summer rush is high).
  • ✔ Prefer hill stations for cooler weather.
  • ✔ Keep hydrated during long journeys.
