Best Places to Visit in Winter ❄️

Snowy escapes and cozy adventures across India.

Top Winter Picks

Gulmarg, Jammu & Kashmir

Gulmarg is famous for skiing, snowboarding, and breathtaking Himalayan views. Route: Srinagar → Gulmarg (by road).

  • Gulmarg Gondola – Asia’s highest cable car
  • Khilanmarg – Scenic valley
  • St. Mary’s Church – Historic landmark

Auli, Uttarakhand

Auli is India’s skiing capital with snow-covered slopes and panoramic views. Route: Rishikesh → Joshimath → Auli (by road).

  • Auli Ropeway – Scenic ride
  • Artificial Lake – Man-made beauty
  • Gurso Bugyal – Trekking spot

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

The Queen of Hills, Shimla offers colonial charm, snowfall, and cozy vibes. Route: Delhi → Kalka → Shimla (train/road).

  • Mall Road – Shopping & cafes
  • Kufri – Snow adventure
  • Jakhoo Temple – Hilltop view

Suggested 4-Day Winter Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Local Exploration

Check-in at hotel, visit Mall Road (Shimla) or Gulmarg Gondola.

Day 2: Adventure & Snow Activities

Enjoy skiing in Auli or snowboarding in Gulmarg.

Day 3: Scenic & Cultural Spots

Explore Kufri (Shimla), monasteries in Gulmarg, or trekking in Auli.

Day 4: Relax & Departure

Leisure morning, shopping for souvenirs, and prepare for return journey.
